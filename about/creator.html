About the Creator

Stephanie Kao is the creator of Data Vizzy. She is currently a data scientist at Accenture Analytics. She attained an early interest in Mathematics and Statistics at Carnegie Mellon University where she savored the geekiness that is notorious at the Pittsburgh campus. After graduating, she moved to Washington, DC where her passion for data visualization grew and became and intellectual fascination.
